Fire Emblem!

Fire Emblem is a fantasy tactical role-playing game franchise developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo. First produced and published for the Famicom in 1990, the series currently consists of thirteen core entries, remakes and spinoff games. It's highly recommend it for fans of tactial games with a historic fantasy storyline. I think all the games are good; however, all are not equal.
